Business Setup Checklist
Once we land on name + colors, this is the launch sequence. Roughly in order.

Phase 1: Foundation

☐ Garage Phase 0 (clear space for workshop)
☐ Move gym to game room
☐ Convert golf-cart port to initial shop
☐ Check HOA deed restrictions (written copy)
☐ Register DBA with Fort Bend County
☐ Texas Sales Tax Permit (Comptroller)
☐ Commercial liability insurance (firearms-specific)
☐ HOA approval for shed structure
☐ Shed design + permits + build
☐ Equipment acquisition (bench, safe, tools)
☐ FastBound software setup (digital A&D book)

Phase 2: FFL Application

☐ Submit ATF Form 7 (FFL application)
☐ Background checks (both applicants)
☐ ATF IOI interview + inspection (~60-90 day wait)
☐ Receive FFL license

Phase 3: Launch

☐ Domain + web hosting (evaluate: Squarespace, WordPress, AWS)
☐ Business email service (e.g. Google Workspace, Zoho Mail)
☐ Build website (landing page + services + gallery)
☐ Google Business Profile (Service Area Business)
☐ Instagram account
☐ Facebook page
☐ YouTube channel
☐ Register on FFL directories (GunBroker, FFL Scope)
☐ TexasGunTalk.com sponsor listing
☐ Business cards for local ranges
☐ First 10 transfer clients (intro rate)

Phase 4: Expansion (Year 1+)

☐ Cerakote capability
☐ Hot bluing capability
☐ Laser engraving services
☐ 3D printing services
☐ Precision rifle assembly
☐ Custom AR upper builds
☐ Ultrasonic cleaning service
☐ Optics leveling/torquing
☐ Type 06/07 FFL upgrade (ammo sales)
☐ SOT / Class 3 (NFA items)
☐ Barrel threading
☐ Action truing / stock bedding
