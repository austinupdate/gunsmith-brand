🎯 Colt Walker Heritage

Texas State Firearm: Colt Walker (1847)
Designed by: Samuel Colt + Captain Samuel Walker (Texas Ranger)
Specs: .44 caliber, 4.5 lbs, 9" barrel
Significance: First military-adopted revolver
Production: Only ~1,100 ever made
Connection: Texas Rangers and cowboy heritage. The Colt Walker was the weapon that defined the Texas frontier — carried by Rangers who kept the peace.

"Our brand ties to this weapon as our Texas identity anchor."

AI Agent Team
Our digital workforce — runs autonomously so we can focus on the craft. Zero employees, 9 agents. Replaces ~2-3 part-time hires (~$30-50K/yr savings).

🤖 Scheduled Agents (run automatically — "runs without us")

🛡 Compliance Monitor
Daily 6 AM. Audits FastBound A&D entries vs work orders, flags missing 4473s, tracks NFA item status, alerts before FFL renewal, TX sales tax quarterly reminder.
📦 Inventory Watchdog
Weekly Monday. Scans ammo/parts stock for low levels, flags backorders, cross-refs wish list with deals, alerts when reloading supplies need reorder.
💬 Customer Follow-Up
Daily 5 PM. Checks open work orders >3 days without status, drafts "your firearm is ready" or "parts on order" messages for approval.
💰 Revenue Tracker
Weekly Sunday. Tallies invoices, calculates P&L, flags unpaid invoices >7 days, tax set-aside reminder (8.25% sales tax + estimated quarterly).
📷 Marketing Autopilot
Mon/Thu. Suggests social posts (before/after photos, "just finished" showcases), gun show calendar reminders, generates content ideas for YouTube + Instagram.
🔍 Auction Agent
Daily. Monitors GunBroker & gunsmith specials for deals matching our wish list — caliber, price threshold, condition grade. Alerts us before we miss steals.

🗨 Chat Agents (invoke on-demand when doing the work)

📈 Shop Estimator
Customer brings a gun in? Ask this agent. Knows the full service menu + pricing, calculates labor + parts, generates a professional PDF estimate.
🛒 Procurement Agent
Need a part? This agent searches Brownells, MidwayUSA, Numrich, and wholesalers. Compares prices. Knows vendor accounts + discount codes.
⚖ Compliance Advisor
ATF questions, "can I do this?" scenarios. FFL regulations, TX state law, NFA rules, interstate transfers. Our legal gut-check before making moves.
🎥 Content Creator
YouTube script writer, Instagram caption generator. Knows our brand voice + colors. Feeds BOTH the gunsmith channel AND personal AI/DIY channel.

⚡ Phased Rollout

Phase 0 (Pre-FFL, now): Shop Estimator + Content Creator — generate quotes for friends/family, build social presence early.
Phase 1 (FFL Approved, Day 1): + Compliance Monitor + Customer Follow-Up — legal obligations start immediately.
Phase 2 (First 90 days): + Inventory Watchdog + Procurement Agent + Auction Agent — stock management becomes real.
Phase 3 (Revenue flowing): + Revenue Tracker + Marketing Autopilot — scale without hiring.
Total: 6 Scheduled + 4 Chat = 10 Agents  |  Startup cost: $0  |  Replaces: ~2-3 part-time employees (~$30-50K/yr savings)

Range Training App
Camera-based live-fire scoring system. Our customers' phones + our branded targets = a training platform we own.

How It Works

1. Customer mounts phone on our branded 3D-printed tripod
2. Camera watches our custom branded target (with QR code)
3. App auto-detects hits, scores placement, tracks times
4. Drills, games, competitions, and progress analytics
5. Social sharing with OUR LOGO on every score screenshot

Physical Products (We Make)

☐ 3D-printed branded phone tripods (~$2-3 to produce)
☐ 3D-printed lane clip mounts (bench/divider)
☐ Branded target variety packs (bullseye, IPSC, drill, fun)
☐ Beginner / kids targets (simpler zones, family-friendly)
☐ Monthly "Target of the Month" (keeps customers returning)
☐ Digital score certificates (PDF, people frame these)

Revenue & Marketing

• QR on every target = marketing funnel to shop page
• Free target pack with every service purchase
• Gun show sales: target packs as physical product
• League nights at partner ranges (monthly events)
• Referral program: share challenge, friend gets free pack
• Every social share = free advertising (our logo on scores)
• YouTube content: film competitions + tutorials
• Range partnerships: they host, we supply targets + app

Build Timeline

Phase 1 (now): Shot timer + drill caller + manual scoring
Phase 2 (pre-FFL): Design target library + 3D print tripods
Phase 3 (post-launch): Camera auto-scoring (computer vision)
Phase 4 (revenue): Games, multiplayer, leagues, range partners

Target Printing Strategy

Prototyping: Home EcoTank printer (letter-size, $0.03/target) for testing designs
Launch: Professional print service (Axle Targets or similar) for branded large-format batches of 100-500
Scale: Bulk orders from Pistoleer/Freedom Gun Targets (2,500+ at $0.25-0.50/ea)
Phase 4: In-house wide-format printer (Epson SureColor, ~$700) for on-demand custom prints
